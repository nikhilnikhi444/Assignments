About GrillHouse
Discover GrillHouse, where culinary artistry meets warm hospitality.
Established in 2019, we are in kochi's go-to destination for explore our foods.
Our chefs, led by Executive Chef Gordon Ramsay, blend tradition with innovation

Our Promise
At GrillHouse, we're committed to quality ingredients, impeccable service, and a cozy ambiance.
Whether you're here for an intimate dinner or a lively celebration, we ensure a delightful experience.

for an extraordinary dining experience.
